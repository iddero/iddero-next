Residencial Velazquez 102

Location and project type

Residential building holding 20 luxury units in C/ Velazquez, in the heart of the Salamanca district, one of Madrid's most exclusive.

Description

With its majestic stone and brick facade, this stately eight floor building breathes the distinctive architecture of Madrid's Salamanca district. It holds 18 apartments with two or three bedrooms, along with two spacious penthouses with solarium and two separate terraces.

Each housing unit is built with the highest quality materials, and equipped with all kind of services and facilities.

The home automation system, based on the KNX standard, includes equipment from Iddero, Niessen and Schneider Electric. Each home is equipped with an Iddero HC2-KNX 7" touch panel that allows users to centrally control all functions, including lighting, blinds, arming and disarming of the security system, technical alarm management, video door phone integration, etc.

Also, thanks to the web server integrated in the Iddero HC2-KNX 7" touch panel, all these functions can be controlled easily from any smartphone or tablet.

This is beyond doubt a reference project where the prime location, the sophisticated elegance of the building, the high quality finishes, and the cutting-edge technology all combine to create unique and unrepeatable spaces.
