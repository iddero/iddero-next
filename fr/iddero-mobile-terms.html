Iddero Mobile — Privacy Policy

General

Iddero Mobile has been developed by IDDERO. IDDERO is a registered trademark of INGELABS, S.L.

Iddero Mobile ("the App") is offered as a free app for mobile devices. It is provided at no cost, and it is intended for use "as is".

This Privacy Policy provides information regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information in connection to the App. If you choose to use the App, then you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not directly collect any Personal Information.

An idderocloud.com account is required in order to use the App. Please refer to the terms and conditions of use of the idderocloud service here.

The App requires use of your phone's Camera in order to scan QR codes for the device linking function. Images captured from the camera are used exclusively for this purpose, and are not stored nor transmitted anywhere.

The App uses a third party crash reporting and analytics solution. Whenever an error is detected during normal use of the App, the App collects data and information on your phone or tablet called "log data". Log data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, hardware model, the configuration of the App when the error was detected, and other statistics.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
